🔒 Security & Privacy Policy

At Saasy Logs, we respect enterprise data security protocols. Because your Salesforce debug logs can contain sensitive logic, object metadata, or system footprints, Saasy Logs does not collect, store, transmit, or view any of your log data.

All processing, parsing, extraction, and string manipulation happen strictly inside your local Google Apps Script runtime architecture. No third-party network requests are ever made with your files. Your data never leaves your personal Google Drive and spreadsheet ecosystem.
